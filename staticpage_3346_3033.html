Governance

The Cowichan Valley is served by each level of government, local, provincial and federal. Various services are available at specific offices.

Federal Services, 211 Jubilee Street, Duncan.
Provincial Services, 5785 Duncan Street, Duncan.
City of Duncan, 200 Craig Street, Duncan.
Municipality of North Cowichan, 7030 Trans Canada Highway.
(Includes Maple Bay, Chemainus)
Cowichan Valley Regional District, 175 Ingram Street, Duncan.
(Includes Mill Bay, Cowichan Bay)
Cowichan Tribes, 5760 Allenby Road, Duncan.
Lake Cowichan, 39 South Shore Road, Lake Cowichan.

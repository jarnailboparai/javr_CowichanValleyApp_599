Accommodation

The Cowichan Valley features a variety of short and long term accommodation options. Visitors can stay in convenient hotels or in one of the bed and breakfasts that might be located at a lake, river or ocean setting. Accommodation options feature prices and features to fit any budget.

Cowichan Valley Inn - located at the north end of Duncan. Featuring pub and restaurant service. Within walking distance to a major shopping mall complex. Across the highway from the Forest Discovery Centre.

Oceanfront Suites at Cowichan Bay - located on the Cowichan Bay waterfront south of Duncan. Within the vibrant marine setting that features plenty of action every day. Restaurant on premises.

Chemainus Inn - located north of Duncan, Chemainus is a popular destination for sightseeing. Experience the charm of an established forestry town that was re-invented with a series of world famous mural displays. Accommodation features indoor pool and is pet friendly.

Castle Cove Inn - located north of Duncan in Chemainus. Featuring a waterfront setting that offers panoramic views from every room.
